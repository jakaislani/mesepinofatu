Skyler Gisondo Notable Filmography

Skyler Gisondo has made a mark in the entertainment industry with his impressive performances in a range of movies. Let’s take a look at some of his memorable roles:

In the critically acclaimed coming-of-age comedy, “Booksmart,” Gisondo played the character Jared, a high school student with a quirky personality. His portrayal of Jared added humor and depth to the film, earning praise from both audiences and critics alike.

2. Licorice Pizza (2021)

Gisondo showcased his acting skills once again in Paul Thomas Anderson’s “Licorice Pizza.” He portrayed Lance Brannigan, a character who becomes entangled in the lives of the film’s main protagonists. Gisondo’s performance was praised for its authenticity and emotional depth.

3. Feast of the Seven Fishes (2019)

In the holiday comedy-drama “Feast of the Seven Fishes,” Gisondo played the role of Tony. The film follows the story of an Italian-American family as they prepare for their traditional Christmas Eve dinner. Gisondo’s performance added charm and warmth to the movie.
